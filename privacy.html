Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

HandyBill ("we," "our," or "us") operates the HandyBill mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted, we cannot read it)
  • Business name and contact information (optional, provided during onboarding)

Business Data

When you use the App, you may provide:

  • Invoice details (client names, amounts, descriptions)
  • Client contact information
  • Expense records and receipt photos
  • Mileage trip data (GPS coordinates when tracking is active)
  • Business profile information

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage analytics (crashes, performance)
  • GPS location data (only during active mileage tracking, with your permission)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's features
  • Create and manage your account
  • Store your invoices, expenses, and business data
  • Track mileage when you initiate GPS tracking
  • Generate reports and tax exports
  • Send password reset emails
  • Improve the App's performance and fix bugs

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase, which provides:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Secure authentication
  • Automatic backups

Your data is also cached locally on your device for offline access. Local data is stored in the app's private storage area.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share data only in these limited cases:

  • Service providers: Google Firebase (data hosting), as described in their Privacy Policy
  • Invoice recipients: When you send an invoice, the recipient receives the invoice content you created
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or government request

5. GPS and Location Data

The App uses GPS location data only for mileage tracking. Location tracking:

  • Only activates when you manually start a trip
  • Stops when you end the trip
  • Is never used for advertising or sold to third parties
  • Can be disabled by denying location permission

6. Receipt Photos

Photos you take or upload for expense receipts are:

  • Stored securely in Firebase Cloud Storage
  • Only accessible to your account
  • Deleted when you delete the expense or your account

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through the App
  • Export your data using the Tax Export feature
  • Delete your account and all associated data from Settings → Delete Account
  • Withdraw consent for location tracking at any time through device settings

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • All personal data is permanently deleted
  • All invoices, expenses, clients, and mileage data are removed
  • All receipt photos are deleted from cloud storage
  • This process is irreversible

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
